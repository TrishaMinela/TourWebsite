School Year Exhibition


Each year, our team will be partnering with Art classes from High Schools around the world to showcase the senior year’s art class final artworks. We hope to give them a major opportunity to have their art be consumed by audiences of different cultures, lifestyle and perspectives. This is a passion project of offering them a true experience in the art world, a step into being confident and more comfortable with their personal art style. Simultaneously, the Artist Feed will benefit from their artistic capabilities. This annual art exhibition will surely give the public and aspiring art students a chance to expose themselves in the Art world.

Contact us to have your school join!

Email: School@TheArtistFeed.com

Annual Artist Exhibition


The website supports influential aspects of the modern period, as well as famous and timeless art styles to convey artistic revolution. Besides being an all-over online art gallery that will constantly change or produce more smaller galleries within, the website also intends to create online annual art shows. With our world’s rapid development and modernization, placing artistic impressions of significant world topics will provide further education. Our world’s insufficiency in the simple knowledge of tourism, culture, religion and of course exposure to art are just a few reasons for these exhibitions to be held.
