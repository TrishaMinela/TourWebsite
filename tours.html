'My Feed' will be a place for the Artist, Trisha Sumiran's collection.
Trisha Sumiran is a 25 year old artist from the Philippines. She was influenced by her family members' artistic sense. She relates art as her escapism, fulfillment of artistic aesthetics, birth of her imagination and a cathartic process. She hopes to explore topics such as individuality, belonging, religion, culture, mental and emotional health and anything provoking and penetrating translated and manipulated into various artworks.

These are a collection of photographs taken by the artist as she traveled through Australia. Colors being slightly manipulated through her feelings within each photograph evokes a sense of longing and melancholy but simultaneously tranquility and fulfillment. A collection of both presence and absence of elements. Photos taken to provoke personal feelings towards humanized locations with it retaining its innate and transcendent beauty. The world stands forcing its pristine state amongst artificial qualities of a human world. Warmth from the trees attract our own warmth through long roads of travels. White rush of waves detract our eyes from the rush of the city. Man-made structures blending with natural structures. We blend as one. We are nature.
